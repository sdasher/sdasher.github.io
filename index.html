About Me

I'm a data analyst and developer with Master's degrees in geographic information systems and climate science. I have worked for schools and environmental non-profits as well as Fortune 50 companies. I love working with, and liaising between, all different sorts of people. I am always developing my technical skills.


I am interested in environmental and educational policy, and I seek out roles related to those causes. I also spend much of my free time serving the Arizona running community, as I have a lifelong love of hiking and running.


Several years ago, I walked from Mexico to Canada on the Pacific Crest Trail.
