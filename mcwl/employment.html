Employment

Working for MCWL

MCWL strives to create a positive work environment while providing competitive pay and benefits. Employees are rewarded for their experience, loyalty, education, and longevity. Above all, we value initiative. Because our job sites are remote, employees are primarily self-monitored. We expect our people to maintain a safe work environment while providing excellent communication and customer service for our clients. Opportunities for advancement are provided for those who excel.

  • Voluntary STD & LTD insurance
  • Voluntary pet insurance
  • Employee assistance program (EAP)
  • Flexible spending account (FSA)

  • Health reimbursement arrangement (HRA)
  • Living quarters provided while on location
