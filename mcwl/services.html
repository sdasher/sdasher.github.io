Formation Logging

Midcontinent Well Logging offers expert mudlogging and drill site analysis using the newest surface instrumentation.

Our standard service includes extremely precise, high-speed Flame Ionization Detection chromatographs (less than 30 second C-5 cycle) that detect even the subtlest gas kicks. We can provide a full suite of geochemical instrumentation for XRF, calcimetry, and digital microscopy. We offer Class 1 Division 1 and 2 rated equipment to meet the highest safety standards on your most hazardous drills.

Real-time drilling parameters and gas curves can be viewed for all wells through our secure web portal. Our geologists interpret lithology and input descriptions in near-real time. We can provide digital and printed mudlogs and reports in a variety of formats and styles.

Geosteering

Midcontinent Well Logging has steered more ultra-deep wells in the Anadarko Basin than any other company. In fact, we pioneered them. Since 2003, we have landed over 1000 successful horizontals in 20 of Anadarko's stacked plays.

Our geosteering geologists work closely with your rig and office personnel to make effective steering decisions around-the-clock. Using rock cuttings, gas shows, gamma ray data, and information from neighboring wells, Midcontinent geosteerers are the regional experts in keeping your well in the payzone. In addition to 24/7 accessibility, our geosteerers issue daily reports detailing apparent formation dip, time spent in zone, hydrocarbon shows and basic drilling data. Included in these reports are our highly detailed geosteering logs.

Whether this is your company's first horizontal, or part of a multi-well campaign at manufacturing speed, our experienced, degreed in-house geologists will work in collaboration with your team from surface to Total Depth.
