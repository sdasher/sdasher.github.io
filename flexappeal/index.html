You Can Always Work Out With Us

Flex Appeal is a shame-free fitness and wellness center for women in all stages of life. We believe that all women deserve to enjoy their fitness journey.

You can work out with us at the gym, or with one of our home workout programs. Daily classes include restorative yoga, Buti yoga, Zumba and HIIT. All of our members receive fitness and nutrition plans customized to their lifestyles and goals.

Come visit us at that gym for a tour. One of our personal trainers will sit down with you for a free, no-sweat, individualized goal-setting session. There's never an obligation to join.

We are excited to grow stronger with you.

Trista Eason, Founder

Trista is a native Texan, retired radio DJ, wife, mother, and avid mountain biker. She is also a certified personal trainer and nutrition coach.

Trista started Flex Appeal in 2015 as a fitness studio where women could push their limits, outside the competitive, body-negative atmosphere of traditional gyms.

Since that time, Flex Appeal has grown into a full-service gym, online community, and safe space, where women of all backgrounds and all fitness levels come together to be empowered through movement.

What is your why?

Many of us have spent a lifetime learning that exercise is a punishment--for laziness, for unhealthy eating, for not being strong enough or attractive enough. Or maybe we feel that exercise is something virtuous that we "should do", so when we skip it, we feel guilt and shame. At Flex Appeal, we believe that the mind and body grow stronger together, and we strive above all to change our members' attitudes toward exercise. By providing the encouragement and the listening ear they need, our members leave the gym feeling proud, hopeful and energized to follow a healthier lifestyle.
